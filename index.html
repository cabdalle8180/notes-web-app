Markdown Tips

  • Headers: # H1, ## H2, ### H3
  • Bold: **bold text**
  • Italic: *italicized text*
  • Lists: - item 1, - item 2
  • Links: [text](https://example.com)
  • Code: `code` or ```code block```
  • Blockquotes: > quoted text
